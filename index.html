Master -ed vs -ing adjectives in minutes!

-ed (How people feel)

"I am interested in this topic."

"She looks excited about the trip."

"He was surprised by the news."

-ing (Characteristics of things)

"This topic is interesting."

"The game was exciting."

"The news was surprising."

Key Rules to Remember

-ed adjectives → Describe how people feel (I feel ___)

-ing adjectives → Describe the characteristics of things/events (This is ___)

✨ Memory Hack: "-ed has a head (person), -ing is a thing!"

😴 → bored (person) 📖 → boring (book)
